Smart Alarm by SOLVER

Solver’s Smart Alarm forces your brain to activate. Instead of simply turning the alarm off, you must complete a small challenge. Solve quick math problems, move your phone, or perform micro-tasks that wake your brain instantly.

This eliminates the snooze habit and helps you start your day with clarity and focus.

Focus Timer by SOLVER

Time is the most valuable resource you have. Every minute you waste is a minute you never get back. The Focus Timer helps you protect your time and use it with intention.

How It Works

In Solver, you simply add the task you want to work on and start the timer. When the task is finished, you stop the timer. Solver automatically records how long you worked on that task, helping you understand where your time goes and giving you better control over your day.
